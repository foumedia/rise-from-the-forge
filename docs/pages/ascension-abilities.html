Unlocking New Abilities

As your crew progresses through the campaign, they will have the opportunity to unlock new ascension abilities. These abilities enhance your crew's capabilities and provide strategic advantages in battles.

How to Unlock Abilities

  • Complete specific missions or challenges.
  • Gain experience points through battles.
  • Participate in community events.

Available Ascension Abilities

Ability Name Description Requirements
Enhanced Combat Skills Increases your crew's attack power. Level 3 Crew
Strategic Maneuvering Allows for better positioning during battles. Complete 5 Missions
Resourceful Tactics Improves resource gathering efficiency. Participate in 3 Community Events
