How to Join the Action

Demo games are available for new players! If you are interested in a demo, please contact @Bastet or @dwillyskilly on Discord.

All campaign coordination happens on our secure and stable Discord platform [LINK]. Simply enter the #fallout channel to connect with fellow players, schedule games, get rules support, or share tales of your battlefield heroics.

A dedicated day to play will occur on Wednesday or Thursdays each week at Fabricators Forge in Coraopolis, PA near Pittsburgh, however due to Factions' flexible format, Players are encouraged to coordinate times to play amongst themselves and may play multiple times a week. (No G.E.C.K. required.)
