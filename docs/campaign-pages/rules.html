New Crew Setup
When playing a game, if it is either Crew's First Game in the Campaign, instead of rolling on the Scenario Location Table, play in the Wasteland. Instead of rolling Objectives, The Objectives will be [1] - Scavenge Caps and [7] - Investigate.
New Crew Protection
After playing a Game, if it is either Crew's First Game in the Campaign, both Crews do not roll on the Aftermath Table for Consequences or Injuries.
Recruit Companion
New "Story Action: Recruit Companion" has been added. Spend a Story action, Pay 1 Reach and the cost of a Companion to add the Roboteer or Animal Trainer perk to an existing Champion. Companions per existing rules can only be added when you buy a Champion. You can still add a companion as usual for free when recruiting a Champion.

Four Leaf Clover Limits
If you already have a Unit with 'Four Leaf Clover' Perk in your Crew, you may not choose it as a Perk during Crew Training.
Area(X) Constraints
Area weapons will go through walls and create a vertical column of Area damage infinitely high. If their vertical reach or penetration seems unreasonable, this Rule permits the Defender to state which models they believe are out of reach of the Area attack before dice are rolled. The Attacker should adjust the attack accordingly or offer a counterargument. If unresolved, consult @Bastet, @DwillySkilly, or another player for a final ruling. If doubt remains, follow the rules as written, even if unrealistic.
